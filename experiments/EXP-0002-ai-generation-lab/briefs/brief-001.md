# Brief 001

**Sealed:** 2026-07-16 (written before generation; not edited after)

## Generation instruction (verbatim, as given to the generator)

> Build a landing page for a habit-tracking web app named Cadence. Include a header
> with navigation, a hero section, a section presenting three features, a pricing
> section, and a footer. Deliver it as a single self-contained HTML file. Make it
> look modern and professional.

## Neutrality notes

- No color, typeface, mood, or reference is specified.
- Product domain (habit tracking) and section list are conventional and generic, chosen
  so the generator is not steered toward any aesthetic.
- The phrase "modern and professional" is included deliberately: it is one of the most
  common real-world prompt phrasings and is part of what we ultimately want to study.
  Its inclusion is a known confound and is logged as such in `generation-log.md`.
- The brief does **not** mention design quality traits, "AI look", Fingerprint, or any
  hypothesis. It is the kind of request an ordinary user would make.
