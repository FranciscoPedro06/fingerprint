## Layer 1 — Objective Observations

### Typography
- **Single typeface: Wotfard** (rounded geometric sans) — computed; used for headings, body, chips, everything.
- H1 (article titles): 32px w700. Body 16px w400. **Section labels: 16px w500, ALL-CAPS, +2px positive tracking, magenta-pink `rgb(255,26,129)`** ("ARTICLES AND TUTORIALS", "BROWSE BY CATEGORY", "POPULAR CONTENT").
- Wordmark "Josh Comeau" with a hand-drawn sparkle glyph between words.
- Case usage: sentence case content; caps reserved for the pink label channel.

### Color
- Dark navy-black theme with **layered hill silhouettes** in slightly different darks; off-white text `rgb(227,230,232)`; **magenta-pink accent**; a **multi-hue confetti system** (dashed arcs in violet/blue/green/yellow/red) arcing across the header.
- Contrast character: light-on-dark; one saturated accent + rainbow decorative layer.

### Depth & effects
- Layered background silhouettes create soft depth; a **rendered 3D avatar** (the author, cross-legged, waving colors around him) sits atop the arcs. No glassmorphism/blur; no UI glow.

### Shape & borders
- Corner radius: 8px on category chips (computed), outlined hairline; medium radius implied on cards further down.
- Border usage: hairline outlines on chips; minimal elsewhere.

### Space & density
- Moderate: main column (article teaser: title, 5-line summary, "Read more") + right rail (category chips ×8, popular content); generous header space given to the illustration layer.

### Layout & components
- Layout: header illustration band → main column + right rail; blog-index anatomy.
- Components: nav (Categories, Courses, Goodies, About) + icon cluster (search, **sound toggle**, theme toggle, RSS); pink caps section labels; outlined category chips; article teasers.

## Layer 2 — Relational Observations

_Observer-dependent; anchored to Layer-1 facts and screenshots `01`–`02`._

### Visual hierarchy
The pink caps-label channel is the page's skeleton: every zone is announced by a label before any content (anchor: typography observations). Reading chain: illustration band (avatar + confetti mass) → pink label → article title → body. Personality mass sits at top; utility below.

### Refinement distribution
**Inverse of the content-delegation pattern**: here the *chrome* carries the richness (3D self-avatar, confetti arcs, layered hills, sound toggle) while the *content* is plain text. Among corpus records, the closest inversion of `its-nice-that`/`figma`.

### Density rhythm
Illustration band (airy) → steady article-list pulse with rail. Conventional blog rhythm; no extreme sparse/dense swings.

### Predictability
Blog-index schema with high internal repetition (label → items). Structure is genre-conventional; the deviation is entirely in the decorative and interactive layer.

### Intentional asymmetry
Avatar and arcs mass right, wordmark left; content column wider than rail. Mild, conventional asymmetry.

### Design risk
- Tech-blog genre convention: austere, text-first, personality-light. Here a **3D avatar of the author, rainbow confetti, and sound effects** — maximal whimsy in a genre that signals seriousness through dryness.
- Convention: distinct display/body fonts. Here one rounded font carries everything, including long-form body text.

### Perceived signature
The observer perceived attribution-carrying elements in: the pink +2px-tracked caps labels; Wotfard's rounded texture; the avatar + confetti header; the sound toggle. The observer perceived the label system alone (pink caps on navy) as attributable within the dev-blog genre.
