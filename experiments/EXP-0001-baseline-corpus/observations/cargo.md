- **Single typeface: CargoDiatypeVariable** (custom Diatype; variable) — computed; no second family in a 150-element sample.
- Wordmark "Cargo Site Builder®": 32px, weight 500, −0.8px at 800px viewport height; **type size scales with viewport height** (the same element renders proportionally huge in the 4800px-tall capture — vh-based sizing, verified across two capture heights).

### Color

### Space & density

### Layout & components

## Layer 2 — Relational Observations

_Observer-dependent; anchored to Layer-1 facts and screenshots `01`–`02`._

### Visual hierarchy
Hierarchy by **solitude**: a single small statement on a void — nothing competes, so the wordmark and the 5 links carry total attention (anchor: 6-link DOM count). No size cascade exists; the hierarchy has one level plus periphery.

### Refinement distribution
All refinement is invested in a single asset: the custom variable typeface and its viewport-responsive sizing behavior. There is no second place refinement could live.

### Density rhythm
None — the page is one beat with no scroll content.

### Predictability
No repeating schema; the page is a single gesture. (Instrument note: predictability-as-schema-count does not meaningfully apply to single-gesture pages.)

### Intentional asymmetry
Wordmark placed off-center left with the right field empty; the emptiness is the composition.

### Design risk
- Genre convention: product homes explain, demonstrate, and convert (features, pricing, demos, social proof). Here the product home is **a wordmark and five links** — everything else is withheld.
- Genre convention: fixed type scales. Here display size is **bound to viewport height**, so the page's proportions, not a type scale, decide the size.
- Same total-refusal family as `mschf`, achieved by **emptiness** where MSCHF used exhaustiveness.

### Perceived signature
The observer perceived attribution-carrying elements in: the small Diatype wordmark adrift on a near-black void; the "[more...]" bracket device. The observer perceived the page attributable chiefly through its refusal — within the site-builder genre, absence of self-demonstration is itself distinctive.
