# Methodology

The rules the project holds itself to, independent of any single experiment. Where the
[Observation Language](../observation-language/) governs how an individual observation is
recorded, the methodology governs how the research around it is conducted.

- [Laws and Principles](laws-and-principles.md) — the non-negotiable commitments and the
  operating principles derived from them.

Methodology changes are consequential and rare. They are recorded here with their
rationale, never applied silently.
