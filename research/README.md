# Research

Synthesized findings and notes that draw **across** experiments and corpus. Where an
experiment is a single apparatus and a corpus record is a single observation, research
artifacts are the cross-cutting analyses: recurrences, comparisons, and open questions.

## What belongs here

- Cross-experiment syntheses and comparative analyses.
- Open research questions and their current state.
- Write-ups intended for an external research audience.

## What does not belong here

- Raw observation records (those are [corpus](../corpus/) / [experiment](../experiments/)
  material).
- Conclusions not expressible as Observation Language attributions grounded in cited
  evidence.
